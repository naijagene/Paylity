# Soft Launch Operations

Use `launch_mode=soft_launch` with `launch_transaction_limit_daily` and `launch_revenue_limit_daily`. Monitor Ops Go-Live Center. Increase caps gradually.

## Launch Vouchers (PAY-033B)

- Airtime-only vouchers reduce product amount only; convenience and gateway fees remain payable.
- Seeded codes: `PAYLITY500` (₦500) and `PAYLITY1000` (₦1,000).
- Manage vouchers in Ops Console → Marketing → Launch Vouchers.
- Track review rate and share rate from the Marketing KPI panel after fulfillment.
